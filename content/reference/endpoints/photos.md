## List photos

List all photos for given account(s).

~~~
GET /photos
~~~

<%= render 'json_response', endpoint: "photos", scopes: [{ public: "public-rentals_write" }] %>

## Get a single photo

Returns a single photo identified by ID.

~~~
GET /photos/:photo_id
~~~

<%= render 'json_response', endpoint: "photos", scopes: [{ public: "public-rentals_write" }] %>
